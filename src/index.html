Do I Wanna Know?



Ever wanted to share some kind of a opinion with someone, but you weren't sure they would accept, or share it? This website was made just for that!


How do I start?

There are two ways you can get an opinion chart to a friend, by making one, or using an existing one. To make your own, click here and fill out the required fields. To use an existing one, click here and fill out a query.
